Membership Options

We offer flexible membership plans to fit your needs, including monthly, annual, and pay-as-you-go options. Members receive unlimited access to our tools and workshops, as well as discounts on special events and classes.

Visit Us

Our makerspace is open to the public during our regular hours. We encourage visitors to stop by, take a tour, and see our space in action. Whether you're a seasoned maker or just curious, we'd love to show you around and answer any questions you have about our community and resources.
